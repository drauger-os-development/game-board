Configuration
game-board currently auto-scales itself to your screen resolution. However, you can manually set the resolution. The manual feature is in place for people with multi-monitor set-ups. 
in the feilds labeled "Width of Monitor" and "Height of Monitor", if you have a multi-monitor setup, please put the resolution of the monitor you wish game-board to be on most often.
